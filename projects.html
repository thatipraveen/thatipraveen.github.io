Selected Projects

These projects represent large-scale, high-impact initiatives spanning platform engineering, privacy governance, global commerce, and AI-powered productivity. Each reflects how I approach ambiguous problems by aligning people, architecture, and execution.

AWS Skill Builder — Enterprise Admin Platform

AWS Skill Builder serves millions of learners globally, but enterprise customers lacked a unified way to manage training, licensing, and learner progress across large organizations.

I led the delivery of a first-party enterprise administration platform, designed as a durable foundation rather than a one-off feature.

My role: Led a 30+ person cross-functional organization spanning engineering, product, and program management.

Key decisions: Treated admin capabilities as platform abstractions, separating enterprise concerns from learner-facing experiences.

Outcome: Enabled enterprise administration for 10M+ learners, reducing administrative friction by ~40%.

Privacy Governance Platform — 700M+ Devices

Amazon’s device ecosystem spans hundreds of millions of active devices worldwide, subject to evolving privacy regulations such as GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.

Fragmented privacy controls made it difficult to reason about compliance, audit data usage, or enforce consistent policies at scale.

My role: Led engineering efforts defining privacy-first architectural patterns in close partnership with Legal, Security, and Compliance teams.

Key decisions: Centralized policy definition while decentralizing enforcement, enabling safe defaults without blocking delivery velocity.

Outcome: Enabled privacy governance across 700M+ devices with 100% GDPR/CCPA/COPPA compliance and improved audit readiness.

Global Purchase & Billing Experience

AWS Training & Certification purchasing involved complex, multi-step checkout flows that created significant user friction, especially across international markets.

The challenge was to simplify the experience while preserving security, compliance, and support for global payment methods.

My role: Led product-engineering partnership across engineering, finance, legal, and security stakeholders.

Key decisions: Simplified workflows end-to-end, reduced duplication, and designed extensible checkout abstractions.

Outcome: Reduced checkout steps from 18 → 6, improved conversion by ~25%, and reduced drop-offs by ~80% across 20+ countries.

AI-Powered Developer Productivity & Automation

As platform complexity increased, developers spent significant time navigating documentation, codebases, and operational knowledge during delivery and incident response.

I led initiatives applying AI and automation to improve developer efficiency while maintaining strong trust and governance guarantees.

My role: Defined strategy and led delivery of AI-powered knowledge platforms and automation capabilities.

Key decisions: Focused on augmenting developer workflows with guardrails rather than replacing human judgment.

Outcome: Improved developer productivity and delivery velocity by ~30% while aligning with responsible AI principles.
